Some Background:

Once thought to be devoid of life, scientists have found large traces of phosphine, a biosignature, high in Venus' atmosphere.
This could either be evidence of life or of an undiscovered geological process.
Venus had long been ignored by space exploration efforts, but it will now likely be the focus of new study.
I think it's really cool how much there is to explore and discover right next to earth!
